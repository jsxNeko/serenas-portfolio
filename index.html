My Story

Hello. I'm Serena. Nice to meet you. I am a Senior at the University of Memphis and graduating 2022 of December with a Bachelors of Science degree in Computer Science. I am passionate about building websites that create an enjoyable experience.

I first found my passion while taking a course called Programming Languages, we had to build our own grammar parser application using Django, after I got the fundamentals down of the backend, I had no idea what to do for the Front End and I did so much research finding out that I can literally do anything from a simple button click to animations. It's what you make it out to be and that's what I love about it. I'm able to make modern or outrageously creative applications with a lot of colors and animations. Fun fact: In my spare time, I create models in Blender3D and make emotes/portraits.
Whether it be very simple to minimalistic or creative and unique. I want to make your users experience easy to manuever without all the fustration. Check out my projects below.
